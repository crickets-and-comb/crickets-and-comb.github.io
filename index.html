About Us

We’re Crickets and Comb — a team who build custom open-source tools for nonprofits, and make sure the folks using them feel supported every step of the way. We care about clarity, ease of use, real-world impact — and keeping things secure, maintained, and steadily improving.

When we build something useful, we share it. Many of our tools grow into resources for the wider open-source community. We stay grounded in collaboration and mentorship, working with people near and far to learn, teach, and create together. It takes care and commitment to keep things running well, and we’re here for it.

Our Projects

bfb_delivery

A Python package and CLI designed to streamline delivery route planning for the Bellingham Food Bank. Provides ETL interface to and from third-party routing service and creates printable manifest spreadsheets to dovetail with legacy workflows. Saves several paid staff hours per week and avoids human error.
